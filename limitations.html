Limitations of Computers

Computers have limitations such as dependency on electricity, vulnerability to viruses, etc.Computers have certain limitations, such as dependency on electricity and technical issues like system crashes or hardware malfunctions. They lack human intuition and creativity, relying solely on programmed instructions. Additionally, they can be vulnerable to security threats like hacking, and their performance may degrade over time due to software and hardware limitations.
