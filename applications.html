Applications of Computer

Computers are used in various fields like education, healthcare, business, and entertainment.Computers have a wide range of applications across various fields. They are used for data processing, communication, entertainment, education, scientific research, and business management. Applications include word processing, graphic design, web browsing, online shopping, gaming, and data analysis, enhancing efficiency and productivity in numerous industries.
