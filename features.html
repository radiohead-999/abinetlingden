Features of Computers

Features like speed, accuracy, automation, and connectivity make computers indispensable.Computers offer several key features, including speed, accuracy, storage capacity, and automation. They can perform complex calculations, store vast amounts of data, and execute tasks without human intervention. Additionally, computers are versatile, enabling multitasking, connectivity, and interaction with various devices and software to improve productivity and efficiency.
