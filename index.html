Definition of Computer

A computer is an electronic device that can process, store, and retrieve data.

Applications of Computer

Computers are used in various fields like education, healthcare, business, and entertainment.

Evolution of Computers

The development of computers over time, from early mechanical devices to modern-day systems.

Features of Computers

Features like speed, accuracy, automation, and connectivity make computers indispensable.

Limitations of Computers

Computers have limitations such as dependency on electricity, vulnerability to viruses, etc.
