Evolution of Computers

The development of computers over time, from early mechanical devices to modern-day systems.The evolution of computers began with early mechanical devices like the abacus and the Analytical Engine. The first generation used vacuum tubes, followed by transistors in the second. The third generation introduced integrated circuits, while the fourth saw microprocessors. Modern computers, based on artificial intelligence, continue advancing rapidly in performance.
